Founded in 2004, Brackenbury Capital is a specialist capital management and advisory firm.

The company originates and structures intensively risk managed alternative asset strategies that seek to provide enhanced investor returns with minimised exposure to global macro uncertainties or unhedgeable tail events.

Our investment managers and partners have a proven track record in delivering value and strong absolute returns from a range of investment classes including equities, real estate, FICC and alternative products. The ethos of all our strategies is to be risk averse while intelligently optimising possible returns with low volatility to our investors.

Based in London and Berlin, the team has a seasoned background in both developing innovative investment solutions and tight risk management combined with deep real estate structuring and capital markets expertise gained at some of the world's leading financial institutions.

Brackenbury Capital is employee owned and independent of any institution or fund management firm.
